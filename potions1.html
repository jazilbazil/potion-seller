ingredients

  • one slice of Glistering Melon
  • a leaf of an evergreen tree
  • deionized water
  • mango juice
  • optionally add a pinch of glowstone dust for extra healing

method

  • pour the mango juice and deionized water in a bowl
  • take the peel off the Glistering Melon
  • finely chop up your leaf and add it to the bowl
  • put in the Glistering Melon and mix (if you don't mix enough your potion will have pulp)
  • once you finish mixing you can optionally add glowstone dust
  • and now your potion is finished
